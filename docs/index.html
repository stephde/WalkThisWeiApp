Features

WalkThisWei is still under development but here are some features that will be included:

Audio Content

WalkThisWei takes the walking experience to the next level, by giving you audio content for landmarks in you environment.

Easy Usage

The user experience in WalkThisWei is designed as easy as possible, in order to make the content accessible for everyone.

Explore the Outdoors

With WalkThisWei exploring your neighbourhood is much more fun and you can also learn a lot while doing so.
